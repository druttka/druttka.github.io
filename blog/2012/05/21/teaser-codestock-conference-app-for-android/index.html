Teaser CodeStock Conference App for Android

21 May, 2012

Today, I found that the CodeStock 2012 schedule has been finalized. Now you know exactly when you can come see me. My first talk conflicts with Leon Gersing’s “You”, and my second with Jim Christopher’s “Raising the Next Generation of Geeks.” I look forward to seeing those of you who don’t fit into their talks in mine!

Anyway, I noticed that they have apps for WP7 and iOS, but not for Android. I’m going to use this as an opportunity to dive into something real as a driver for my MonoDroid series. I’m too tired to do a full, detailed post right now, but the hacky beginnings of my discovery / learning journey have appeared on GitHub.

It’s been fun so far; I’m looking forward to taking it further (and cleaning it up - a lot of cleaning it up).
