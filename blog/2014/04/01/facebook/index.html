Facebook?

1 April, 2014

I’ve quit Facebook a few times. The last time was very successful until one day, I was working with Joey, and Joey said…

Hey, looks like {kid’s name}’s ballet class was pretty great!

And I said

Wat?

##My Current Facebook Policy Only one friend, my wife, so I can enjoy posts and pics of her and my kid.

##The Dilemma

I know Facebook is wrong. I know going back is a trap, but…

People at work are having happy fun joke time and I’m missing it.

I’m considering revocation of my policy, and jumping all the way back in.

Someone talk me down from this ledge.
